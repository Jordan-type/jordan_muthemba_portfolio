- **Solidity**: My go-to for writing efficient, secure smart contracts.

- **Web3.js & Ethers.js**: Essential for integrating blockchain functionality into front-end applications.  

### **Front-End Development**

- **TypeScript**: With over {new Date().getFullYear() - 2018} years of experience, TypeScript is my primary language for scalable applications.
